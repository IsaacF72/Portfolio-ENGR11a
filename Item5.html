A simple game

TicTacToe

For this part, I chose to remix a tic-tac-toe board. I used Fusion to hollow out each hole in the board, as well as to make the X pieces a little more unique by hollowing out their centers. Initially, I encountered a mild issue with converting the meshes to solids, but once I resolved that, the rest was relatively straightforward. I learned how to use the hole tool and other tools. I printed it out, utilizing a raft; however, upon removing it from the raft, I found that the X pieces didn't fit in the holes. Later on, I learned about tolerance in 3d printing, which explained it.
