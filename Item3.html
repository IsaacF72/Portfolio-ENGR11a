Milk Carton Opener

45~ minutes

This is a 3D-printed milk carton opener that fits around the cap of the milk carton, making it easier to open for people with weak grip strength. The design was made by Tony Glynn on makers making change. The documentation was pretty simple to understand; however, if I were to make some changes to it, I would make it more textured, as in its current iteration, it is smooth. It may be easier to turn if it's textured similarly to milk cartons.
